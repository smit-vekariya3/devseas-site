About DevSeas Global

Empowering businesses worldwide with premium export solutions, international market expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality and reliability.

Who We Are

DevSeas Global Pvt. Ltd. is a leading export company specialising in premium chemicals, ingredients, and industrial solutions. With years of experience in international trade, we have built deep expertise in global market regulations, logistics, and supply chain management.

We deliver safe, compliant, and high-performance ingredients designed to elevate the quality of pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, cosmetic, and food formulations. With precision-driven sourcing and a quality-first mindset, DevSeas Global is your dependable partner for superior excipients and cosmetic ingredients.

From our offices in Vadodara, India and Berlin, Germany, we operate across 40+ countries — serving industries ranging from industrial manufacturing and agro chemicals to food processing and personal care.
